Read This (Important)


- Read all the Terms and Conditions of the Contract as deployed by the Landlord before signing the Contract. If any error is found contact the landlord to draft a new contract accordingly.

- Registration Fee of the Contract is paid by the Landlord at the time of Contract Deployment and is transferred to the Owner of the Contract (Government) immediately.

- Maintain sufficient balance in your MetaMask Ethereum Wallet as Tenants are required to pay the Security Deposit as mentioned in the Contract when signing the contract.

- The Security Deposit will be held in an Escrow Account and when Tenant and Landlord will provide approval, will be transferred to the Tenant back after deduction of the decided amount.

- If you wish to accept the Contract, first Sign the Contract and then pay the Security Deposit.
